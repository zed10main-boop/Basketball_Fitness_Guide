4. Conditioning & Recovery

**Focus:** Building the stamina to maintain high effort and quick decision-making late in the game, and ensuring muscles repair efficiently.

Full-Court Sprints

**Goal:** 6-8 sets

Sprint at maximum effort from baseline to baseline, then jog back slowly for recovery. This replicates the intensity and rest periods of a fast break.

Jump Rope Intervals

**Goal:** 5 minutes high-speed intervals

Focus on short, fast bursts (30 seconds work, 30 seconds rest). Great for foot speed and low-impact cardiovascular fitness.

Dynamic Warm-up (Pre-Training)

**Goal:** 5-10 minutes

Perform light, movement-based stretches like leg swings, arm circles, high knees, and butt kicks *before* every workout to prepare muscles.

Static Stretching (Post-Training)

**Goal:** 10 minutes

Hold deep stretches for 30-60 seconds on major muscle groups (hamstrings, hip flexors, quads) *after* your workout to improve flexibility and aid recovery.

3. Lower Body & Core Strength

**Focus:** Building foundational strength for power generation, deceleration (stopping quickly), and stability during contact.

Barbell/Dumbbell Squats

**Goal:** 3 sets of 8 reps

The cornerstone of leg strength. Focus on keeping your chest up and hitting parallel or slightly below. This directly translates to vertical jump power.

Bulgarian Split Squats

**Goal:** 3 sets of 10 reps per leg

Excellent for unilateral (single leg) strength and balance, mimicking the staggered stance used in jumping and running.

Romanian Deadlifts (RDLs)

**Goal:** 3 sets of 10 reps

Strengthens the hamstrings and glutes, which are critical for sprinting, jumping, and injury prevention.

Plank Variations

**Goal:** 3 sets, 60 second hold

Focus on tight, stable core muscles (front plank, side plank). A strong core transfers power from the legs to the upper body for shooting and hitting.

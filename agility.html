2. Speed & Agility Drills

**Focus:** Quick acceleration, deceleration, and efficient change of directionâ€”essential for beating defenders and quick defense.

T-Drill

**Goal:** 4 sets (Sprint, shuffle, shuffle, backpedal)

Use four cones in a 'T' shape. Focus on staying low when shuffling and planting hard for quick turns.

5-10-5 Shuttle (Pro Agility)

**Goal:** 4 sets

Sprint 5 yards, change direction and sprint 10 yards, change direction and sprint 5 yards back to start. Improves lateral quickness and explosive cuts.

Defensive Slides

**Goal:** 3 sets of 30 seconds

Practice fast lateral movement in a low, defensive stance. Ensure your feet never cross and you maintain your center of gravity.

Agility Ladder Drills

**Goal:** High Knees, Lateral Shuffle (3 sets each)

Focus on light, quick feet and coordination. Use the high knee run and the two-in, two-out lateral shuffle to build fast footwork.

1. Power & Vertical Jump Drills

**Focus:** Maximize jump height (vertical explosion) and ensure safe landing mechanics to prevent injury. These are plyometric exercises, which should be done with maximum effort and perfect form.

Box Jumps

**Goal:** 3 sets of 5 reps

Focus on landing softly on the box with bent knees to absorb impact, then step down. Do not jump down.

Depth Jumps

**Goal:** 3 sets of 5 reps

Step off a small box, absorb the landing, and immediately explode into a vertical jump. The quicker the ground contact, the better.

Lateral Bounds

**Goal:** 3 sets of 8 reps per leg

Explosive side-to-side hops. Push off hard with one leg and land softly and catch your balance on the other. This builds lateral power for side movements.

Standing Broad Jumps

**Goal:** 3 sets of 5 reps

Maximize horizontal distance by swinging your arms and exploding forward. Important for horizontal bursts like the first step or a fast break.

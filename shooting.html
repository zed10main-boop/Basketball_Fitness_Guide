5. Shooting Drills & Tips

**Focus:** Consistency, practice volume, and proper mechanics. Hit these volume targets in your shooting sessions.

Daily Shot Volume Goals

  • **Layups:** Shoot 50 layups (Alternate hands/sides, practice speed).
  • **Free Throws:** Shoot 70 free throws (Focus on consistent rhythm).
  • **Mid-Range:** Shoot 40 shots from the elbows/short corners (Practice pull-ups and catch-and-shoot).
  • **Three-Pointers:** Shoot 10 shots from beyond the arc (Focus on form and legs).

Technique Tips

  • Follow-Through: Make sure you have the **follow-through** during the shooting motion to increase your accuracy at the rim. Finish with your hand in the cookie jar!
  • Power Source: **Bend your knees** when shooting long-range shots to increase the force of your shot. Power comes from the legs, not the arms.
  • Consistency is Key: **Practice and practice** that will improve you every day. Everyday is a progress as long as you train and practice.
